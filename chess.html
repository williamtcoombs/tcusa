0. Blueprint

Spend sixty percent of your time on endgame and middle game, leaving forty percent for openings. Focus on a few openings that are inherently sound structurally, are not as dependent on move order, and where there is less memorization required. As White, consider the London System. As Black, pick a response to 1.e4 (e.g., Petrov Defense, Caro-Kann Defense, French Defense) and something for 1.d4 (e.g., Slav Defense, King's Indian Defense).
