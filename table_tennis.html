1. The Grip

We will focus on the shakehands grip, but the penhold grip certainly has some advantages worth considering. Ben Larcombe provides insight into grip nuance based upon several months of experimentation, especially how the label shakehands is intuitively potentially misleading because it diverts attention to the lower fingers when perhaps the focus should be on the pinching action between the thumb and forefinger, what he calls "the relaxed shakehands grip."
